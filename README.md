1. Purchasing or Ordering Market Research Reports
On this website, clients can purchase ready-made statistical reports or order custom market research on foreign markets. The "Pricing" section shows that for $1,000, the company analyzes competitors, investors, and market size, delivering the final report to the client within 7 business days.
2. Registering for the Accelerator (Online Accelerator)
This is the core section of the website. The Accelerator is a short-term 3-month (or 8-week) educational and business program for entrepreneurs. Through this program, companies can:
 Study online courses.
 Meet with international experts to refine and improve their projects.
 Finally, pitch their projects to foreign investors during "Demo Day" to secure funding and investments.
3. Submitting Applications (Lead Generation / Lead Capture)
Since these services are expensive and complex, users do not buy them instantly with a credit card like in a regular online store. That is why buttons like "Apply Now", "Submit Application", or "Become a Partner" are placed throughout the website.
 When a client fills out their name, email, and phone number in the final form, this data is automatically sent to the website owners' CRM system.
 Sales managers (like Fatima, whose photo is shown in the mockup) then call the clients to close the deal and sell the services.
